About

Elena Vasquez

Brooklyn, NY

I’m an independent web designer who believes the best digital experiences are the ones that feel inevitable — like they couldn’t have been designed any other way. My work sits at the intersection of branding and technology, where visual identity meets interaction.

Before going independent, I spent four years at Pentagram working across brand, editorial, and digital projects. That experience taught me to think in systems and to sweat the details that most people never notice but everyone feels.

I work with studios, startups, and creatives who care about craft. If you have a project that needs to look and feel exceptional, let’s talk.
