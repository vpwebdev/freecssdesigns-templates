FAQ

What denomination is Sanctum Church?

Sanctum is a non-denominational Christian church. We hold to historic Christian beliefs while welcoming people from all backgrounds.

Is there childcare available?

Yes! We offer nursery care for infants through age 2, and programming for children ages 3-12 during all Sunday services.

Can I watch services online?

Absolutely. All Sunday services are livestreamed and archived on our Sermons page.
