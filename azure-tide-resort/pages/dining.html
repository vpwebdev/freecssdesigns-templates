Culinary

Dining

Three distinct restaurants, one philosophy: the freshest coastal ingredients, prepared with artistry, served with the Atlantic as your backdrop.

Fine Dining · Seafood

Saltstone

Our flagship restaurant where the ocean sets the menu. Chef Adrienne Calloway works directly with local fishermen and coastal farmers to create an ever-evolving tasting menu that honors the Atlantic's bounty. The seven-course experience is paired with wines from our curated coastal cellar.

Dinner: 6:00 PM – 10:30 PM · Reservations required
Reserve a Table
Grill · Casual

Driftwood Grill

Relaxed, open-air dining right on the sand. Driftwood serves wood-fired seafood, prime cuts, and seasonal salads in a barefoot-friendly setting. Come for lunch after a morning on the beach, or linger over sunset cocktails as the sky turns golden.

Lunch & Dinner: 11:30 AM – 9:30 PM · Walk-ins welcome
View Menu
Bar · Small Plates

Horizon Bar

Perched on the resort's rooftop, Horizon Bar offers craft cocktails infused with tropical botanicals alongside refined small plates. The 360-degree panoramic views make this the definitive spot for sunset drinks and late-evening conversation.

Daily: 4:00 PM – 12:00 AM
