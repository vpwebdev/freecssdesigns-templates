The Coastal Spa

Drawn from the Sea

Our 8,000-square-foot spa draws its philosophy from the ocean itself. Each treatment incorporates marine minerals, sea botanicals, and the natural rhythm of the tides to create experiences that are as deeply restorative as they are luxurious.

The facility includes eight treatment rooms, a thermal circuit with plunge pools, a salt inhalation chamber, and a meditation terrace overlooking the water. Whether you seek deep-tissue relief or quiet contemplation, the Coastal Spa offers a path back to balance.
