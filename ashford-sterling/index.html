About the Firm

A Legacy of
Excellence

Founded in 1998 by partners Victoria Ashford and Robert Sterling, our firm was built on a singular principle: every client deserves counsel that is as thorough as it is tenacious.

Today, Ashford & Sterling is a team of 18 attorneys serving clients throughout California. We combine big-firm resources with the personal attention and accessibility of a boutique practice.
