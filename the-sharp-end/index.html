Our Story

More Than a Haircut.
It's a Ritual.

"We opened The Sharp End because every man deserves a place where the cut is precise, the conversation is real, and the experience is something you actually look forward to."

Founded in 2012 in the heart of San Francisco, The Sharp End started as a two-chair shop with a simple mission: bring back the art of the barbershop. No gimmicks, no rush — just skilled barbers, quality products, and an atmosphere that feels like home.

Today we're a team of six barbers, each with their own specialty and style. Walk-ins always welcome, but booking ahead guarantees your spot with your preferred barber.
