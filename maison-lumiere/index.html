Our Heritage

Four generations of mastering the art of brilliance

Born in the ateliers of Antwerp and perfected across decades, Maison Lumiere began with a simple conviction: that every stone carries a story, and every setting should honor it.

Today, our master jewelers blend centuries-old techniques with contemporary vision, creating pieces that transcend fashion and become part of your family's legacy.

Savoir-Faire

The Art of Our Craft

From raw stone to radiant jewel, every step is guided by passion, precision, and an uncompromising eye for perfection.

Stone Selection

We hand-select each gemstone from the world's most trusted sources, evaluating cut, clarity, color, and carat with unyielding precision.

Design & CAD

Our designers merge hand sketches with cutting-edge 3D modeling to perfect every angle before a single tool touches metal.

Master Setting

Each stone is set by hand under 10x magnification, ensuring flawless alignment and maximum light performance.

Final Polish

Every surface is hand-finished to a mirror sheen, with each piece undergoing our rigorous 37-point quality inspection.
