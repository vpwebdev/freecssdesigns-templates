Our Story

Three generations of winemaking

Founded in 1987 by Jean-Luc Moreau, Domaine Noir began with a single acre of Pinot Noir vines and an uncompromising vision. Today, our estate spans 45 acres of sustainably farmed vineyards across the Willamette Valley's most prized terroir.

Each vintage reflects our philosophy: minimal intervention, maximum expression. We believe great wine is made in the vineyard.
