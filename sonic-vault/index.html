About Sonic Vault

Built by audio engineers,
for your stage.

Sonic Vault was founded in 2016 by a collective of touring sound engineers who were tired of inconsistent rental experiences. We built the inventory we wished we'd always had access to — meticulously maintained, rigorously tested, and ready to perform.

Every piece in our warehouse is cleaned, firmware-updated, and function-tested between rentals. We don't just rent gear — we deliver reliable sound with white-glove service, from a team that has mixed on the stages you're building.
