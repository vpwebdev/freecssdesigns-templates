The Experience

More Than A Festival

Seismic Sound

Custom-built PK Sound system delivering bone-rattling bass across 4 unique stages. Feel frequencies you didn't know existed.

Immersive Visuals

360° projection mapping, laser labyrinths, and LED installations that transform the desert into another dimension.

Desert Camp

Premium camping experiences from tent villages to luxury glamping under the stars. Wake up to sunrise yoga and bass.

Art Installations

Large-scale interactive art pieces from world-renowned artists scattered throughout the festival grounds.

Food Village

Over 40 food vendors featuring cuisines from around the world. Vegan, vegetarian, and gluten-free options throughout.

Wellness Zone

Recharge at our dedicated wellness area featuring massage, meditation sessions, and chill spaces away from the bass.
