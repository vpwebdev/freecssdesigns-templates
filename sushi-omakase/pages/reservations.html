Before You Book

Our counter seats twelve. We offer two seatings each evening, Tuesday through Saturday. Reservations are confirmed by email within 24 hours.

Policies

● A credit card is required to hold your reservation. No charge is made at booking.
● Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations are subject to a $100 per person fee.
● Guests are asked to arrive 5 minutes before their seating time. Late arrivals may forfeit their reservation.
● We are unable to accommodate outside food or beverages. Corkage is not available.
● Children under 10 are not permitted at the counter. We apologize for the inconvenience.
