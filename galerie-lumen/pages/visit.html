FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is photography allowed in the gallery?
Non-flash photography is permitted for personal, non-commercial use in the permanent collection galleries. Special exhibition spaces may have photography restrictions. Please check with gallery staff upon arrival. Tripods and selfie sticks are not permitted.
Do you offer guided tours?
Yes, we offer complimentary guided tours every Saturday at 11:00 AM and 2:00 PM. Private tours for groups of 10+ can be arranged by contacting our education department. Audio guides are available for rent at the front desk in English, French, and Spanish.
Is the gallery wheelchair accessible?
Galerie Lumen is fully wheelchair accessible. All exhibition spaces, restrooms, and the gallery shop are accessible via ramps and elevator. Wheelchairs are available to borrow free of charge at the front desk. Service animals are welcome.
How do I become a member?
Membership starts at $75 per year for individuals and $120 for dual memberships. Benefits include unlimited free admission, exhibition previews, member-only events, and a 10% discount at the gallery shop. Visit the front desk or contact us by email to join.
Can I host a private event at the gallery?
Our gallery spaces are available for corporate events, private dinners, and celebrations. The main exhibition hall accommodates up to 200 guests, and the mezzanine lounge is perfect for intimate gatherings of 30-50. Contact our events team for availability and pricing.
Do you accept artwork submissions?
We review artist portfolios twice yearly, in January and July. Please submit a portfolio of 10-15 works, an artist statement, and CV to submissions@galerielumen.com. Due to volume, we can only respond to submissions that align with our curatorial program.
Is there parking available?
Validated parking is available at the Arts District Garage on 7th Street, a 2-minute walk from the gallery. Street parking is available but limited. We encourage visitors to use public transit — the gallery is a 5-minute walk from the Arts District Metro station.
