Our Story

Founded in 2018, Galerie Lumen has become a beacon for contemporary art that challenges, inspires, and transforms.

The Beginning

A space for contemplation

Galerie Lumen was born from a simple conviction: that great art deserves to be experienced, not merely observed. Our founder, Margaux Delacroix, envisioned a space where the architecture itself would become part of the artistic dialogue.

Housed in a converted industrial warehouse in the arts district, the gallery's raw concrete walls and soaring ceilings provide a dramatic backdrop for works that push the boundaries of contemporary practice.

Today, we represent over thirty artists working across painting, sculpture, photography, and digital media, with a permanent collection of more than two hundred works.

Our Mission

We believe that light reveals truth. Every exhibition at Galerie Lumen is curated with intention — to illuminate perspectives that might otherwise remain in shadow, to elevate emerging voices alongside established masters, and to create encounters between viewer and artwork that linger long after leaving.

Art is not a luxury. It is a necessity. We commit to making contemporary art accessible, thought-provoking, and deeply human.
